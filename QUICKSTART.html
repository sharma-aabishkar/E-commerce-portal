üìñ How to Use

1. First Time Users

  1. Click "Sign Up" button in top navigation
  2. Fill in your details (name, email, phone, password)
  3. Accept terms & conditions
  4. Click "Create Account"
  5. You'll be redirected to login page

2. Login

  1. Click "Login" button
  2. Enter your registered email and password
  3. Click "Login" button
  4. You're now logged in!

3. Shopping

  1. Go to Products page
  2. Browse all available products
  3. Click "Add to Cart" to purchase
  4. Click "Add to Wishlist" to save for later
  5. Use filters and sort options to find products

4. Reviews

  1. Go to Products page
  2. Scroll to "Customer Reviews" section
  3. Select a product from dropdown
  4. Choose a star rating
  5. Write your review (minimum 10 characters)
  6. Click "Submit Review"

5. Contact Us

  1. Click "Contact Us" in navigation
  2. Fill in your name, email, subject, and message
  3. Click "Send Message"
  4. View FAQs for quick answers

üîç Form Validation Rules

Registration/Login:

  • Email must be valid format (example@domain.com)
  • Password must be at least 6 characters
  • Passwords must match in registration
  • Phone number must be 10 digits
  • All required fields must be filled

Contact Form:

  • Name cannot be empty
  • Email must be valid
  • Subject cannot be empty
  • Message must be at least 10 characters

Review Form:

  • Product must be selected
  • Rating must be selected (1-5 stars)
  • Review must be at least 10 characters

üé® Customization Tips

Change Colors:

Edit the CSS variables at the top of style.css:

  • --primary-color: #ff6b35; (Orange)
  • --secondary-color: #004e89; (Dark Blue)
  • --accent-color: #1ac8eb; (Cyan)

Add More Products:

Edit the sampleProducts array in script.js and add new product objects with id, name, price, description, category, and rating.

Change Store Name:

Search for "TechMart" and replace with your store name in all HTML files.

‚ùì Troubleshooting

Not seeing changes?

  • Clear browser cache (Ctrl+Shift+Delete)
  • Hard refresh (Ctrl+F5)
  • Close and reopen browser

Local Storage not working?

  • Check if browser allows local storage (settings/privacy)
  • Not supported in private/incognito mode
  • Try a different browser

Form validation not working?

  • Open browser console (F12) to check for errors
  • Ensure JavaScript is enabled
  • Check browser compatibility
